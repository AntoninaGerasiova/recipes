Ingredients:
- 2-4 cucumbers
- 2 tomatoes
- 6-8 garlic cloves
- lettuce
- 8 middle mashrooms(champignons)
- handful of pignoli nuts
- hard cheese
- prefered veg oil
- salt

Directions:
- chop garlic cloves with slices and champignons with slices or in dice
- fry garlic and champignons in oil over high heat
- fry pignoli nuts without oil over low heat
- chop cucumbers, tomatoes, lettuce to the big bawl
- grate the cheese or cjp it with slices
- add  pignoli nuts, and garlic with champignons
- salt and add veg oil
- mix it
